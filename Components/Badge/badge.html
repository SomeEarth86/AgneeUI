Badge
Badge are little circular icon located at one of the corner of avatar. It conveys information like presence of user (status such as online, busy or DND) or numeric information such as notifications etc.
Profile Badge
The profile Badge shows the status of user. Here we are taking 3 status namely online, idle and DND using classes online | idle | dnd respectively. 
                    
                        <!-- Online Badge -->
<div class="badge">
    <img class="avatar" src="https://rb.gy/mfpksz" alt="avatar" >
                        
    <div class="badge-display online"></div>
</div>


                        <!-- Idle Badge -->
<div class="badge">
    <img class="avatar" src="https://rb.gy/mfpksz" alt="avatar" >
                        
    <div class="badge-display idle"></div>
</div>


                        <!-- DND Badge -->
<div class="badge">
    <img class="avatar" src="https://rb.gy/mfpksz" alt="avatar" >
                        
    <div class="badge-display dnd"></div>
</div>
Number Badge
The number badge refers to the no of items that are selected in the parent object (wishlist, notifications etc.).
