Image
Image are one of the most crucial part of any project. It represents the pictorial representation of the essence that application wants to convey.
Responsive Image
Images can be made reponsive by adding res-img tag. 
                    
<div class="img-box">
    <img class="res-img" src="https://rb.gy/pbahrk" alt="goku-img">
</div>
Round Image
Images can be made round by adding round-img tag. 
                    
<div class="img-box">
    <img class="round-img" src="https://rb.gy/pbahrk" alt="goku-img">
</div>
